Ugali with Sukuma Wiki

A staple East African meal combining cornmeal porridge with sautéed kale or collard greens.

Ingredients

For the Ugali:

  • 2 cups white cornmeal (maize flour)
  • 3-4 cups water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

For the Sukuma Wiki:

  • 1 large bunch kale or collard greens, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 large onion, finely diced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup water or broth
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice

Instructions

For the Ugali:

  1. Bring 3 cups of water to a boil in a heavy-bottomed pot.
  2. Add salt to the boiling water.
  3. Gradually add the cornmeal while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  4. Keep stirring as the mixture thickens. Add more water if it becomes too thick.
  5. Reduce heat to low and continue to cook for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. The ugali is ready when it pulls away from the sides of the pot and forms a firm dough.
  7. Turn it out onto a serving plate and shape into a round or oval mound.

For the Sukuma Wiki:

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add onions and sauté until translucent, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  4. Stir in cumin and coriander, cooking for 30 seconds.
  5. Add tomatoes and cook until they soften, about 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add the chopped greens and stir to coat with the spice mixture.
  7. Pour in the water or broth, cover, and reduce heat to medium-low.
  8. Simmer for 5-7 minutes until the greens are tender but still bright green.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Finish with a squeeze of lemon juice before serving.

Serve the sukuma wiki alongside the ugali. Traditionally, a small piece of ugali is broken off and formed into a small depression with your fingers, then used to scoop up the sukuma wiki.

Nutritional Information

Per serving:

  • Calories: 350 kcal
  • Protein: 8g
  • Carbohydrates: 65g
  • Fat: 7g
  • Fiber: 8g
  • Sodium: 320mg
